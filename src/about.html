Our Team

Matthew Messina

Chief Marketing Officer

Matt is responsible for establishing new supplier relationships to ensure NY General Wholesale's ability to offer competitive prices. As the current owner of a high-volume Snyder’s Pretzel route, Matt has the unique experience of building brick-and-mortar store sales and developing and maintaining excellent client relationships. Matt dove into the wholesale industry in his early twenties by creating his first wholesaling company with his brother, Christopher. His fascination with the industry continues to expand as he finds new and innovative ways to source and sell merchandise. Aside from work, Matt is a travel junkie, video game lover, and food gastronome.

Daniel Messina

Chief Executive Officer

Daniel established and currently operates a successful acupuncture clinic; providing relief to patients with orthopedic maladies since 2016. Using valuable lessons learned from building his own business, Daniel has partnered with his brothers to turn their mutual dream into reality, creating NY General Wholesale. To this day, Daniel develops short- and long-term strategies for the advancement of their company. These strategies have empowered NY General Wholesale personnel to thrive in a team centric environment through the cultivation of a pragmatic solutions driven corporate culture.

Christopher Messina

Chief Operating Officer

Chris was first hired in 2013 as a warehouse associate and was quickly promoted to manager within 6 months. Chris’ research and innovative changes to the standard company procedures nearly doubled the companies output. After gaining significant inside knowledge about the industry, Chris and his brother Matt decided to create their own spin-off company enabling him to eventually break off from his warehouse employer. Having no experience in the retail industry, he quickly gained a working knowledge and hands on experience by purchasing a Snyder's Pretzel distribution business in 2017. He immediately observed major issues within the Direct Store Delivery (DSD) industry. This knowledge allowed for NY General Wholesale to penetrate the market and grow a business by solving these issues.
